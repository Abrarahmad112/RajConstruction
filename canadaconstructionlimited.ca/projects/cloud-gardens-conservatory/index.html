The Cloud Gardens Conservatory is located in the busy downtown core sitting in the Bay Adelaide Centre. The conservatory was renovated in 2014 and re-opened in spring 2015. Canada Construction upgraded the conservatory with a new climate control system, shade curtains and grow lights to address the shading caused by newly built office towers. Improvements also included a renewed misting system and a new monorail system to help maintain the mechanics and vertical wall gardens. Other improvements such as structural work, electrical and painting were completed.
